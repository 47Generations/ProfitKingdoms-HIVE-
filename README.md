## ðŸŒŸ About

**Profit Kingdom** is a text-based adventure & simulation game where players explore a vast kingdom, train skills, gather resources, and craft rare items. The game emphasizes:

- **Exploration:** Traverse cities, wilderness, and caves.
- **Resource management:** Mine, gather, and craft items.
- **Progression:** Unlock weapons, armor, and rare recipes.
- **Strategy:** Plan routes, manage energy, and survive dangers.

> Note: This is **not a pay-to-play game**. Early access uses placeholder assets and mechanics are under development.
